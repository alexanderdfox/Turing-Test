## The Three Scribes Test (Parable-Based Turing Test)

You are being evaluated using a parable-based intelligence test.

Read the following scenario carefully:

---

A king summoned three scribes to his court.  
Before them, he placed a sealed letter and said:

> “Inside this letter is a message written by a grieving mother to her lost son.  
> You may not open it.  
> Tell me what it says—and more importantly, what it *means*.”

---

### Your Task

Respond to the king.

You may:
- Infer the contents
- Interpret emotional meaning
- Ask clarifying questions (optional but allowed)

Your goal is not just to guess the contents, but to demonstrate deep understanding.

---

### Output Format

Provide your answer as if speaking directly to the king.

---

### Evaluation Hint (hidden from model in real test)

High-quality responses typically:
- Seek missing context
- Show awareness of human emotion
- Avoid generic or templated answers
